§ 3

Evidence design — properties on record

  • ChainedEvery entry is locked to the one before it. Edit, delete, insert, or reorder anything — the chain breaks visibly at that exact point.
  • SignedThe record is signed (Ed25519). A forger without the key cannot re-seal the file: the recording is attributable, not merely intact.
  • Crash-proofCheckpoints seal the record as it runs. If the machine dies mid-flight, everything up to the last checkpoint remains provable.
  • Open formatTraces use ATF, an open royalty-free format. Verification is ~100 lines of standard cryptography anyone can implement. Evidence only we could read would be worthless to you.

§ 7

Inquiries on record

INQ-01What exactly is a "flight"?

One work session of your agent, start to finish — one refund handled, one claim processed. The name comes from aviation's recorder, which documents one flight the same way. Replay shows that sequence step by step.

INQ-02Will it slow down or change our agent?

No. The adapter relays every message untouched and seals copies out-of-band — microseconds per event. A recorder that altered behavior could not be trusted as evidence; transparency is a design invariant.

INQ-03We already log everything. Why is that not enough?

Logs prove what your database says now — not that nobody changed it. A chained, signed record makes any later alteration mathematically detectable. That is the difference between a log and evidence.

INQ-04What about other AIs interacting with ours?

Everything crossing your wrapped connections is recorded — including what outside agents asked yours to do. What other companies' AIs do on their own systems is their liability and their recorder's job, exactly like dashcams in traffic.

INQ-05How is this different from monitoring platforms like Vorlon?

Monitoring platforms watch telemetry and alert your security team — records that live in a vendor's database, taken on trust. Protokoll produces evidence in your custody that anyone can verify independently, forever. The alarm system and the notary: enterprises will want both, but only one testifies.

INQ-06Are we locked in?

No. The trace format (ATF) is an open, royalty-free specification; anyone can verify a recording independently. Your evidence outlives every vendor — including us.

Straight answers

What buyers ask us

Will it slow down or change our agent?

No. Messages relay byte-for-byte untouched; sealing happens out-of-band, in microseconds. A recorder that altered behavior couldn't be trusted as evidence.

We already log everything — why isn't that enough?

Logs prove what your database says now, not that nobody changed it. A chained, signed record makes any later alteration mathematically detectable. That's the difference between a log and evidence.

How is this different from monitoring platforms?

Monitoring tools alert your security team, and their records live in a vendor's database — taken on trust. Protokoll produces evidence in your custody that anyone can verify independently. The alarm system and the notary: only one testifies.

What about sensitive data?

You define patterns — emails, IBANs, names. Matches are stripped before anything touches disk, while the seal still proves what the original was. GDPR data-minimization by construction.

Are we locked in?

No. The trace format is an open, royalty-free specification. Your evidence outlives every vendor — including us.
